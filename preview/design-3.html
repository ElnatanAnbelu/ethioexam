A car accelerates uniformly from rest and reaches 20 m/s in 8 seconds. What is the acceleration of the car? Grade 9 · Motion in a Straight Line

✓ Correct — the answer is B (2.5 m/s²) Use a = (v − u) / t = (20 − 0) / 8 = 2.5 m/s². Since the car starts from rest, u = 0.
